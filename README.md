## Why This Template?

A PDF resume is a snapshot. An online resume is a **living document** that works for you around the clock:

- **Job seekers** who want one shareable link instead of emailing file attachments that get lost
- **Career changers** who need a clean, professional presence that doesn't scream "developer" or "academic"
- **Freelancers & consultants** showcasing experience, skills, and social proof to potential clients
- **Anyone applying** who wants to stand out from the stack of identical Word docs on a recruiter's desk

> *"I put the link in my LinkedIn headline and started getting inbound messages within a week. Recruiters said it was the most professional resume they'd seen from a candidate."*
> — **Elena Ruiz**, Product Manager

> *"Updated my resume in 5 minutes by editing a Markdown file and pushing to GitHub. No reformatting, no broken layouts, no 'which version did I send them?' panic."*
> — **James Okafor**, Solutions Architect

## FAQ

<details>
<summary><b>Do I need to know Hugo?</b></summary>
No. Edit visually with Ownable CMS or write Markdown. Hugo is the build engine — you don't need to touch it.
</details>

<details>
<summary><b>Can I export to PDF?</b></summary>
Yes. Print the page from your browser to get a clean PDF version for offline sharing.
</details>

<details>
<summary><b>Can I host for free?</b></summary>
Yes. GitHub Pages, Netlify, Vercel, and Cloudflare Pages all have free tiers for static sites.
</details>

<details>
<summary><b>Can I migrate my content later?</b></summary>
Yes. Your site is just Markdown files in a folder. Copy them anywhere.
</details>

<details>
<summary><b>What's Hugo Chat?</b></summary>

</details>
